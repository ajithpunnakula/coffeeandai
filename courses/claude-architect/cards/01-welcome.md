# Welcome to Claude Architect Exam Prep

This course prepares you for the **Claude Certified Architect -- Foundations** exam, Anthropic's first official technical certification for solution architects building production applications with Claude.

## Exam Format at a Glance

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Questions | 60 multiple-choice |
| Time limit | 90 minutes |
| Passing score | 720 / 1000 (72%) |
| Scenarios | 4 of 6 randomly selected |
| Cost | Free for first 5,000 partner employees |

## The Five Exam Domains

The exam is organized into five weighted domains. Every question maps to one of these domains, and your score is weighted accordingly:

1. **[[Agentic Architecture & Orchestration]]** (~25%) -- Agentic loops, multi-agent orchestration, hooks, session management, and task decomposition. This is the highest-weighted domain and the focus of Domain 1 in this course.
2. **Tool Design & MCP Integration** (~20%) -- Tool descriptions, structured error responses, MCP configuration, and built-in tools.
3. **Claude Code Configuration & Workflows** (~20%) -- CLAUDE.md hierarchy, custom commands and skills, plan mode, CI/CD integration.
4. **Prompt Engineering & Structured Output** (~20%) -- Explicit criteria, few-shot prompting, tool_use for structured output, validation-retry loops.
5. **Context Management & Reliability** (~15%) -- Progressive summarization risks, context positioning, escalation patterns, error propagation.

## How This Course Works

This course covers all five domains with interactive cards including:

- **Page cards** that teach core concepts with exam-relevant detail
- **Quiz cards** that test your understanding with exam-style questions
- **Scenario cards** that walk you through multi-step architectural decisions mirroring the real exam
- **Reflection cards** that prompt you to synthesize what you have learned

The exam also includes **6 real-world scenarios** (you will see 4 of them). Each scenario places you in the role of an architect making decisions for a production Claude application. We will practice all 6 scenarios so you are prepared no matter which 4 appear on your exam.

## What to Focus On

The exam heavily tests your ability to distinguish **correct architectural patterns** from **anti-patterns**. There are 18 documented anti-patterns across the 5 domains -- 10 of them are marked critical. Learning to spot these distractors will let you eliminate 2-3 wrong answers before even reading the correct one.
