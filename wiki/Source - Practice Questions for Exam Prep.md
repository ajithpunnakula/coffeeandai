This source is the practice questions page for the [[Claude Certified Architect Exam]], providing 25 exam-style questions modeled after the real exam format with detailed explanations. The questions can be filtered by domain: Agentic Architecture (6 questions), Tool Design and [[MCP]] (4 questions), [[Claude Code]] Config (5 questions), Prompt Engineering (5 questions), and Context and Reliability (5 questions).

The distribution of questions across domains roughly mirrors the exam weighting, with Agentic Architecture having the most questions. The page functions as an interactive quiz that candidates can start and work through, with each question including detailed explanations to reinforce understanding of correct answers and why incorrect options are wrong.

The practice questions complement other preparation resources including the 12-week study plan with weekly practice tests, detailed domain breakdowns, the exam guide with format details, and the exam FAQ. Together these resources form a comprehensive preparation ecosystem for the certification published on claudecertifications.com.
