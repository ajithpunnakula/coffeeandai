This source presents a structured 12-week study plan for the [[Claude Certified Architect Exam]], designed to take candidates from fundamentals to exam-ready by spending roughly one hour per day (approximately 84 hours total). The plan is divided into three phases: Foundations (Weeks 1-4), Applied Knowledge (Weeks 5-8), and Exam Prep (Weeks 9-12), covering all five exam domains with 13 practice tests integrated throughout.

The Foundations phase covers agentic loops and the core API (Week 1), multi-agent orchestration with hub-and-spoke architecture and [[Subagents]] (Week 2), [[Hooks]] and workflows including PostToolUse hooks and session management (Week 3), and tool design with [[MCP]] configuration (Week 4). Each week follows a consistent pattern of daily study topics culminating in a 10-question practice test, with a rest day built in for review.

The Applied Knowledge phase shifts to [[Claude Code]] configuration and the [[CLAUDE.md File]] hierarchy (Week 5), plan mode and CI/CD integration using the `-p` flag and [[Message Batches]] API (Week 6), prompt engineering with few-shot prompting and structured output via tool_use (Week 7), and validation-retry loops with multi-pass review strategies (Week 8). This phase emphasizes practical application of concepts like TDD iteration patterns, session context isolation, and batch processing for cost savings.

The Exam Prep phase focuses on context management including progressive summarization risks and the "lost in the middle" effect (Week 9), advanced context topics like information provenance and human review with stratified sampling (Week 10), hands-on integration exercises covering multi-tool agents, team workflow configuration, data extraction pipelines, and multi-agent research (Week 11), and final exam preparation with three full 50-question practice exams (Week 12).

The source also provides study tips emphasizing the importance of reading the official exam guide first, focusing on anti-patterns (since exam distractors are often anti-patterns), building real projects with the [[Agent SDK]] and MCP servers, deeply reviewing wrong answers, using the [[Anthropic]] Academy on Skilljar, and understanding architectural reasoning rather than just memorizing facts. The plan links to companion resources including the exam guide, domain deep dives, anti-patterns cheatsheet, scenario walkthroughs, and practice questions.

- Weekly cadence with daily tasks and integrated practice tests
